ABOUT



My name is Danny 'Haus' Martin

I'm a creative.

At the moment, I'm working on releasing my first video game, The Puppeteer. It's a real challenge making a game from scratch but a lot of fun too! I also make lot of art - mainly drawings that describe how I feel in a particular moment.
